About Me

I am a software architect and systems engineer, and I am the CEO of Lael Learn and Free People are non-profit organizations, while C1YPHER, Offl1n3, Lael Labs, Lael Therapeutics, and Lydia Press are for-profit entities.

I have decided to dedicate my life to helping free people from modern-day atrocities such as surveillance, data collection, think tanks, etc. Nearly everything I do aligns with this singular vision. In the future, I plan to devote a considerable amount of time to open-source initiatives, and I strive to donate whatever I can to projects that empower others to gain freedom.

On this blog, I will cover various areas of technical interest, which you can explore in the categories.
